Welcome

ARTEmission is an international art academy, based in London, UK. Although our centre is in London we are an international art school with the multi-national team of able tutors. Art is a global phenomenon and that’s why online lessons that cannot be affected by borders, travel or visa restrictions are very important to us. Having said that, we are offering an in person classes as well.

Our mission

Lessons at ARTEmission will be mostly conducted in English, due to the international nature of the school. However, our tutors also speak Chinese, Russian and Georgian and therefore lessons could be conducted in these languages, if required.

Students are accepted from the age of 12 and upwards.

Classical, quality education that stems from knowledge of Ancient World, Mythology and Art History is something we strongly believe in. Young people should be given an opportunity to expand their horizons in order to grow into open-minded, tolerant and whole individuals. That is why at ARTEmission all art forms will be represented.
