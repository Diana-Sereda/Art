Dimitry Devdariani

Craft of actor, directing, playwriting

Arts Career

  1. 2022 Directed “It’s Not All Shrovetide for the Cat” Alexander Ostrovsky. Performed at The Tea House Theatre, London in June 2022.
  2. 2021 to date Drama tutor at Cambridge Russian Academy (CRA)
  3. 2021 Directed “Little Tragedies, Part 2” by Alexander Pushkin. Performed at The Tea House Theatre, London in July 2021
  4. 2020 Directed “Little Tragedies, Part 1” by Alexander Pushkin. Performed at The Tea House Theatre, London in March 2020.
  5. 2019 to date Ongoing workshops in the craft of actor
  6. 2019 Directed “The Man with the Flower in His Mouth” by Luigi Pirandello. Performed at The Tea House Theatre, London in December 2019.
  7. 2019 Adapted for stage and directed “The Little Greyneck” by Mamin-Sibiryak. Performed at The Courtyard Theatre, London in January, 2019.
  8. 2018 Directed “Playing Turgenev”, based on two short plays by Ivan Turgenev. Performed by Russian Classics Theatre at The Courtyard Theatre, London in November, 2018.
  9. 2018 Directed “Moments of Truth” by Gareth Pilkington. Performed in collaboration with North London Actors at The Courtyard Theatre, London in October, 2018.
  10. 2018 Directed and. Performed in a dramatised reading of “The Cease Fire” Written by Tatiana Dittrich and. Performed at Rossotrudnichestvo, London.
  11. 2018 Directed and adapted for stage “Demonic Woman” by Nadeszda Teffi. Performed for The Stars of Albion International Arts Festiival at Rudolph Steiner Theatre, London.
  12. 2018 Directed 6 Monologues by Gareth Pilkington A dramatised reading by North London Actors, working towards a full scale production in September, 2018.
