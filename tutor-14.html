Brian Hosefros

Composer


As a composer, I strive to express, with emotional honesty, what cannot be said through mere words.

Catalogue of Compositions and Commissions since 2017 –

In Progress:

  • Peter Prickles – an operetta for children Story, Libretto and Music by Brian Hosefros; Commissioned by El Paso Youth Opera for Spring 2025, and family-opera-series Utah Opera for Spring 2024. First semi-staged-partial-performance April 22th, 2022 by the College of Idaho opera department.
  • Creation [working title] – a chamber puppet opera in collaboration with Linde Schnike and Vox Muziek Theatre, Netherlands.
  • The Ballads of Warpants Rolphus – a puppet opera for adults, Story, Libretto and Music by Brian Hosefros, Puppet Design by Jason Southgate. Interest from Scottish Opera, Glasgow; Opera Holland Park, London; Irish National Opera, Dublin.
  • The Adventures of a Fox, a Chicken, and a Robot -- a puppet opera for children, in collaboration with the University of Connecticut opera, theatre and puppetry departments., USA 2023 – 2025.
  • Absurd Songs, set 2 – words and music by Brian Hosefros. Commissioned by Nadia Aguillar
  • Viola Sonata – Commissioned by Aurora Torres (USA), Jeremy Gurchenkov (Russia/UK) and Melinda Blackman (UK) for Spring 2020 performances (postponed due to COVID-19). Recommissioned for completion by Tina Wagner 2022.

Completed:

  • Adaptations of Gregorian Chants from the Romanum Gradule – for the St. Paul’s Community Boys’ Choir
  • The Canticle of Daniel – commissioned by the Diocese of Boise for Easter service 2020. Performance postponed due to COVID-19. Performance set by St. Michael’s, Boise 2023.
  • A Montserratian Fairytale – Story and Music by the children of the Montserrat Youth Choir (2018/19), edited by Brian Hosefros, Catherine Dorsette and Amelia Daley. Performance March 9th, 2019
  • Letters to the Child, a song cycle for oboe and tenor in three parts – words by Brian Hosefros. Originally commissioned by Michal Rogalski, 2018. First performance by Connor Vrooman Spring 2023 for Clarinet and Tenor.
  • Absurd Songs, set 1 – words by Brian Hosefros. Commissioned by Jordan Bowman. Performed in New York, May 28 2018.
  • Solo Female Student Dance – Commissioned by Natalia Kremen Ballet School for the 2018/2019 School year.
  • The Tale of Wasted Time, incidental music – commissioned by Goodge Street Theatre Group, 2017/2018 season.
  • The Hummingbird – libretto by Leo Doulton. Commissioned by Guildhall School of Music and Drama as part of the MA Opera Making Course, a partnership with the Royal Opera House. Premier June 6th 2017. Chamber Opera.
  • Strekoza I Muravej (Grasshopper and Ant) -- libretto by Vadim Yurchenko accepted for performance at the Tête à Tête Opera Festival and Grimeborn Opera Festival, in London; December 2012 – August 2013. It won second place in 2013 Flourish Opera Competition
