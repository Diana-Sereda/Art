Anastasia Cherkasova

Filmmaking

Director, director of photography Saint Petersburg State Univesity of Cinema and Television Tetrahedron St Petersburg, St Petersburg City, Russia

2003 – present “Tetrahedron” films studio. In 2006 she made the 1st documentary film “With much love and kisses”. The documentary attended 11 international film festivals and received prizes in Poland and in Moscow for the best first film director.

From 2000 till 2008 worked as director of photography and script-writer for the All-Russian State Broadcasting channel “Culture”. In 2005 the 2 cycles of documentary films “Small museums of St. Petersburg” and “St. Petersburg: time and place” received the State Prize.

In 2018 she completed her 2nd feature length documentary “Landscape after the storm”.

Filmography:

  • “Forgotten tango”, Russia, 1999. Fiction. Director of photography.
  • “Chase after Cinderella’”, Russia, 2000. Fiction. DP.
  • “Saint-Petersburg: time and place”, “Small museums of St. Petersburg”, Russia, 2000 – 2008.
  • Cycles of documentary TV films for the channel of “Culture”. Director of photography, script-writer. .
  • “With much love and kisses”, Russia, 2006. Documentary. Director & director of photography.
  • “Cabale”, France, 2008. Documentary. DP. “Against the current”, Russia, 2009. Documentary. DP.
  • «La City, la finance en eaux troubles», France, 2011. Documentary. DP.
  • «L’Affaire de tous», France, 2011. Documentary. DP.
  • “Ombres sur le Saint-Siege”, France, 2013. Documentary. DP.
  • “The first world war. The unknown war”, Russia, 2014. Documentary. Editor.
  • “Easter”, Russia, 2013. Fiction film. Director of photography. The film attended the “Global Russia” program at Cannes film festival in 2014.
  • “Interpol. Le nouveau ordre mondial”, France,2016-2017. Documentary. DP.
  • "Landscape after the storm", 2018. Documentary. Director & DP.

Education:

  • 1997. St. Petersburg University of Cinema and TV
  • 1995. St. Petersburg Academy of Culture

Trainings:

  • 1999. Co-production Course at Baltic Media Centre (Bornholm, Denmark).
  • 1999. “East meets West” and “Docs without frontiers” (summer school for documentary film producers). (Ebeltoft, Denmark)
